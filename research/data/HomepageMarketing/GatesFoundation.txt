Our mission is to create a world where every person has the opportunity to live a healthy, productive life. The Gates Foundation is a philanthropic organization that works to dramatically reduce disease and poverty and build a more equal world. We invest our expertise and resources in organizations, research institutions, and global alliances that are fueling progress toward a future that is free of infectious diseases, provides economic opportunity for all, and ensures the health of mothers and babies. Our charitable support focuses on innovative products, ideas, and approaches that have the potential to save and improve the greatest number of lives. We fund and bring together people and organizations in the global health and development sectors so we can collectively accelerate progress on urgent issues. We pursue our goals by providing funding to those who have the experience and expertise to make an impact. The Gates Foundation was founded on a simple premise: that people everywhere, regardless of identity or circumstances, should have the chance to live healthy, productive lives.
