Privacy Policy
CogBias AI (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1 Information We Collect
Account Information: Name, email address, login method (via Google/Firebase).
Usage Data: How you interact with the platform (e.g., features used, rephrasing frequency).
Text Inputs: Texts you submit for bias detection are processed but not stored unless explicitly saved by you.
Payment Data: Handled securely via Stripe. We do not store your full payment details.

2 How We Use Your Data
To provide and improve the CogBias AI service.
To analyze trends and user behavior (in aggregate).
To send occasional updates, with your consent.

3 Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. We may share anonymized data with research partners or for product development.
Legal requirements: We may disclose personal data if required by law or to protect our rights.

4 Your Rights
You can request deletion of your data at any time. You may opt out of marketing communications. You can access and update your information from your account dashboard.

5 Data Retention
We retain minimal personal data for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.
